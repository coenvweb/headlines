Walking the SciComm walk with P. Sean McDonald

You can’t help but notice that P. Sean McDonald, lecturer in the College’s Program on the Environment, places a high value on science communication. Not only does he walk the walk himself, but he encourages his students to as well. We sat down with McDonald to talk about why he values science communication, how it relates to his own work and how he helps others become better communicators as well.
